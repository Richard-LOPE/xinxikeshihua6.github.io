周边产品设计:卡片

1834年

T.Davenport

发明了世界上第一辆真正意义上的电动汽车，尽管其采用的是不可充电的简单玻璃封罐蓄电池驱动..
The world's first true electric car was invented, albeit powered by a simple, non-rechargeable glass-sealed battery..

1881年

G.Trouve

装配了以铅酸电池为动力的电动汽车，成为世界上第一辆以可充电电池为动力的电动汽车..
It is equipped with an electric vehicle powered by a lead-acid battery, making it the world's first electric vehicle powered by a rechargeable battery..

1897年

J.B.Entz

美国费城电池公司首席工程师J.B.Entz制造了第一辆油电混合动力电动汽车..
J.B. Entz, chief engineer at the Philadelphia Battery Company, built the first diesel-electric hybrid electric vehicle..



1899年

Lohner-Porsche

德国人波尔舍开发了Lohner-Porsche电动车.是世界上第一辆四轮驱动的电动车..
The German Porsche developed the Lohner-Porsche electric car. It is the world's first four-wheel drive electric vehicle..

20世纪70年代

Oil crisis

中东爆发的石油危机迅速蔓延至全球.靠燃油生存的普通汽车第一次面临着新的挑战..
The oil crisis that broke out in the Middle East quickly spread across the globe. For the first time, ordinary cars, which survive on fuel, face new challenges..

20世纪90年代

Rise

电动汽车的研究开发再次进入一个活跃期.电动汽车能逐渐媲美燃油汽车..
The research and development of electric vehicles has once again entered an active period. Electric vehicles are gradually comparable to gasoline vehicles..
